VN Flight Radar

Live ADS-B aircraft radar · Waveshare ESP32-S3-Touch-AMOLED-1.75

Flash the firmware straight from your browser — no toolchain, no PlatformIO.

Use Chrome or Edge on desktop (Web Serial required). Serve this page over HTTPS to flash.
Installs firmware v__FWVER__
  1. Plug the board into your computer with a USB-C data cable.
  2. Click Install and pick the USB JTAG/serial or USB Serial port.
  3. Choose Erase device + Install on first flash.
  4. When it reboots, join the VNFlightRadar-Setup WiFi from your phone.
  5. Fine-tune anything later at http://vnflightradar.local/.
